Delicious Chinease Noodles Receipe

April 05, 2023

Chinease Noddles

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Yields: 4 Servings
For Begginers

01.

Cook the Noodles: Follow the package instructions to cook the noodles until they are al dente. Drain and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Toss the noodles with a little vegetable oil to prevent sticking and set them aside.

02.

Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper (if using). Set the sauce aside.

03.

Stir-Fry the Ingredients: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for about 30 seconds or until fragrant.

04.

Add the sliced vegetables and your choice of protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the protein is cooked through.

05.

Combine Everything: Add the cooked and drained noodles to the skillet along with the prepared sauce. Toss everything together and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the noodles and evenly coat them with the sauce.

06.

Serve: Transfer the Chinese noodles to serving plates or bowls. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Chinese noodles!
